Eventify



"Clubs and organizations let students congregate around a common cause, interest or course of study at a time when social connections are crucial"




Eventify provides a paltform for student communities and collages to add details/information about upcomming events ;a join initiative of a group tech-enthusiastic of collage students who wish to explore various oppurtunities ahead of them. Eventify aims to bring together information of all possible events,hacathons,webinars at one single platform. Students gain real-world experience and practice important habits like leadership, problem-solving, time-management and offers students the opportunity to apply their classroom learning to practical scenarios that have real implications.
